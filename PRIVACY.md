# Privacy

Your data stays with you. Free Workout Timer doesn't collect anything about how you use it.

- Your timers are saved on your phone and never sent to any server.
- No accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no tracking, no ads.
- The app's only network activity is an automatic check for app updates. It carries no information about you. The only system-level prompt that ever appears is the standard App Store / Play Store review request.

If that ever stops being true, this file will say so.
